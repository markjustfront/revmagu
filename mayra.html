Mayra, Keeper of the Vinyl

Story and Concept Art by Mar Aguilar Serrats

Mayra is a normal girl, but she has run into some trouble. As a creative suffering from burnout, her search for inspiration leads her to a mysterious vinyl record. What begins as a quest for ideas quickly becomes a journey through demons, angels and the secrets of life, death and destiny itself. If she saves the world in the process, that's just an added bonus.

Season 1

01

The Disc, The Shop and That Thing

Mayra, a creative writing and illustration student, discovers a mysterious vinyl disc that becomes bound to her soul. Now she must evade the demons sent by Satan, who seek the disc capable of freeing their master from Hell.

02

The Angel Has a What Now?

After nearly being overwhelmed by the dangers surrounding the disc, Mayra is rescued by Cornelious, a mysterious angel with an unusual approach to solving problems.

03

Step on a Crack

Strange cracks begin appearing throughout the city. When Mayra accidentally steps on one, small demonic creatures escape from the depths below.

04

Salty Much?

After witnessing Cornelious destroy a demon with salt, Mayra enthusiastically embraces this new weapon, without fully understanding its limitations.

05

The Side Job

Demons discover where Mayra works and attack her during a shift. Cornelious must help her protect both her life and her job.

06

Getting Results

Mayra begins using her supernatural experiences as inspiration for her final project. While her professor is impressed, she starts feeling watched.

07

Relearning the Rules

Misunderstanding her bond with the disc, Mayra throws it away and is forced to chase it across the city while keeping Satan's minions from reaching it first.

08

Blind Spot Society

Cornelious introduces Mayra to the hidden world that exists alongside humanity. New allies, new dangers and the truth behind horseshoes await.

09

Policeman, It's All a Mistake

A demon impersonates Mayra and causes chaos around town. The result: Mayra ends up behind bars trying to prove her innocence.

10

Salem in 2023

Rumors spread and Mayra is accused of being a witch. Meanwhile, questions surrounding Cornelious's true identity continue to grow.

11

Finders Keepers

Mayra discovers an injured demonic creature and decides to help it. Convincing Cornelious that keeping it is a good idea proves much harder.

12

Bird's Out of the Bag

During a battle, Cornelious suddenly disappears. His past, his arrival on Earth and his role in Heaven are finally revealed.

13

13

As Tuesday the 13th approaches, Mayra senses disaster coming. Together with James, she prepares for what could be the end of the world.

14

Mirror, Mirror on the Wall

Mayra learns that mirrors can be dangerous gateways. A simple accident creates a terrifying new threat.

15

Juno

A determined conspiracy theorist begins investigating Mayra. Keeping Cornelious's existence secret becomes increasingly difficult.

16

God, Why?

Cornelious revisits painful memories from his past and decides to confront God about the choices that shaped his life.

17

Double Digits

After endless bad luck and supernatural disasters, fortune finally seems to smile upon Mayra.

18

It's All in Your Head

A mind-altering demon infiltrates Mayra's thoughts, forcing her to confront her fears, insecurities and darkest ideas.

19

You're Staying With Me

The aftermath of recent events leaves Mayra shaken. Cornelious is acting strangely and the future seems more uncertain than ever.

20

At Dusk

Satan launches a final assault during twilight. Mayra and her allies must defend the disc through the night in a battle that will decide everything.
